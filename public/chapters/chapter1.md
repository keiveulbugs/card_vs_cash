# Why SmartPay?

Compared to its European peers, Germany stands out for its high reliance on cash. Many shops limit card or digital payment by setting minimum purchase amounts or do not offer cashless payment options at all. 

To understand why, we spoke with shop owners in Munich. The most common reason mentioned were **perceived high costs of card payment providers.** However, the **real costs behind cash**, such as from counting and transporting money, were often underestimated.
