# Cost Calculator and comparison
### It can be hard to decide which Point-of-Sale payment operator to choose. They have different benefits, and different subscription models. To help you out with this decision, we made an easy to use calculator to compare the major payment operators with each other, and using cash only.

<br><br>

#### The companies shown in the calculator are:
<br>

- **SumUp**: Offers various subscriptions. No Commitment and Plus are their subscription for small shops. Above €35,000 monthly revenue, companies are eligble for reduced fees.
- **Unzer**: Offers two payment terminal subscriptions, POS Go and 5000. The first one is free to start with, but has a fixed percentage of transaction costs. While the second one has a monthly fee of €22.90 with fixed transaction costs of €0.07 for regular debit cards. For credit cards and non-European card this number is higher.
- **Zettle**: Offers a card payment terminal for an one time fee of €29. On top of this there is a recurrent percentage fee of 1.79% for all types of cards. The company is part of PayPal
- **Concardis**: Offers variety of subscriptions and terminals. You can rent or buy 5 different terminals from them. There is a montlhy rate of €2.99 and a yearly rate of €250.00 (the latter is waived when at least €25,000 a year is paid through the terminal). For regular European debit cards the transaction costs are €0.02 + 0.065%.
- **Adyen**: Offers a single subscription which costs €0.13 + 0.60% per transaction for regular European debit cards. However, this percentage can go up to over 4% for credit card payments depending on the card issuer.
